The article examines how brands should decide between AI avatars and human creators for marketing campaigns, arguing that synthetic media excels at cost-efficient top-of-funnel engagement while human authenticity drives conversions. The most effective strategy combines both: AI for global reach and human talent for trust-building and sales closure.
